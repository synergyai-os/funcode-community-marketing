## Post copy

**Everyone can create. Learn how to build with AI.** (**STR-1**)

We're starting an online community for product people who'd rather poke at a working prototype than polish another PRD. (**STR-1**, **AUD-1**)

**Build with AI** — our name for it — means shipping prototypes through agent governance and structured memory, not slide decks and slow iteration. (**GLO-3**) You tinker with agents until something real exists. Good enough ships. The rest gets deleted or handed off. That's the job. (**STD-4**, **STR-2**)

**Who this is for:** product builders who are agent-curious, iterate on specs today, and want to learn by doing — not by watching someone else's toolchain demo. (**AUD-1**) You don't need our stack to belong. FunCode is product- and framework-agnostic; we share patterns for building good products with AI, not a mandatory tool religion. (**INS-11**, **DEC-5**)

**Everything here is free.** Watch, read, discuss, create an account, start threads, moderate — every step on the engagement ladder, no paywall to belong or learn. (**DEC-5**, **GLO-9**, **INS-15**) Revenue later comes only from optional paid tools and services you choose — never from locking the community behind a membership fee. (**DEC-9**)

**One idea we're building around: the Play Room.** (**GLO-2**, **INS-5**) Pull requests aren't a production gate — they're a sandbox where everyone works in the open. Review signals serious intent to merge value, not permission to start building. We picked that up from Fiona Fung on Lenny's Podcast and we're running with it in our own repos. (**INS-5**, **LAND-1**)

We're figuring this out in public — same repo, same Chain, same messy Play Rooms as the rest of you. (**WP-1**, **STD-4**) Randy's been building communities since age twelve; FunCode is the next chapter, not a guru launch. (**INS-12**)

---

**Join the community — free.** Watch, read, discuss, and build alongside product people. No paywall to belong, learn, or participate. (**STR-7**, **DEC-5**)
