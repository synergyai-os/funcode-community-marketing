# Everyone can create

**Learn how to build with AI.**

FunCode is a community for people who'd rather ship a prototype than write another PRD. Whatever you want to make — a product, a tool, a small idea you can't shake — you can build it now, with AI doing the heavy lifting and you steering.

## What "Build with AI" means here

You ship real prototypes through agent governance and structured memory, instead of long documents and slow iteration. It's product-agnostic and framework-agnostic. We're not here to sell you a stack — we're here to help you build good products with AI: for yourself, your team, or your organisation.

## One idea that changes how it feels

Stop treating the pull request as a gate you have to earn your way through. Treat the codebase as a **Play Room** — a sandbox where everyone works, and review just signals you're serious about merging something valuable. (Borrowed from Fiona Fung on [Lenny's Podcast](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ybrl4FYM57c).)

## It's free — all of it

Watch, search, read, study, try things, start a discussion, help someone else — whatever level you want, you get. If you want it, it's yours. Later, if you'd like tools and services to make a living doing work you love, we'll have paid products for that. The learning never costs anything.
